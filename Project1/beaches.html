hukilau and ehukai

The image on the left is Hukilau Beach, in Laie, a picturesque coastal spot known for its serene atmosphere and beautiful, golden sands. Perfect for swimming, picnicking, and beachcombing.
The image on the right is Ehukai or Pipline Beach, located across Sunset Elementary. My friends and I love tanning, playing spikeball/volleyball, and playing in the big waves here.
