aloha

The North Shore of Oahu is a haven of natural wonders and cultural richness, and I am incredibly fortunate to have grown up here. Growing up here meant being surrounded by the beauty of nature and the warmth of a tight-knit community. Whether it was getting pounded by waves in Sunset, attending highschool football games, or simply enjoying food at Hauula's 7/11, there's always something fun to do around here.
Here are some places I love to go to:

  • Hauula's 7/11
  • Sunset Beach
